01

Information we collect

None. BowlsKnockout does not collect any personal information, usage data, analytics, device identifiers, location data, or any other information from you or your device.

We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, advertising identifiers, or any third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.

02

How your data is handled

Anything you enter into the app — such as player names or draw configurations — stays on your device. It is not sent to us, to any server, or to any third party.

If you choose to print, share, or export a draw using your device's built-in share sheet, that action is handled entirely by iOS and goes directly to the destination you choose.

Your draws never leave your iPhone or iPad unless you explicitly share them.

03

Third-party services

BowlsKnockout does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking services.

04

Children's privacy

Because the app does not collect any data, it does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in the user's jurisdiction).

05

Changes to this policy

If this privacy policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date. Since the app does not collect data, any future changes are likely to be minor wording updates.

06

Get in touch

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, we'd be happy to answer them. Email is the fastest way to reach us.
